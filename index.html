About Me

I'm a product manager who specializes in building data-driven systems that turn complex operational challenges into scalable optimization opportunities.

My career has taken me from hardware and consumer goods to digital consulting, sports data, and now to one of the largest consumer-facing companies in sports betting. Along the way, I've developed a unique perspective on how to build products that bridge business strategy, technical systems, and real-time decision-making.

Today at DraftKings, I lead Trading Intelligence products that help the sportsbook make better decisions faster—optimizing pricing, margin application, risk allocation, and operational efficiency across thousands of markets and millions of bets.

Current Work at DraftKings

As Senior Product Manager for Trading Intelligence, I build products that systematize optimization decisions to improve unit economics at scale.

Pricing & Margin Optimization

Developing systems that dynamically adjust pricing and margin strategies based on market conditions, customer behavior, and competitive positioning to maximize profitability without unnecessary volume loss.

Risk Allocation & Decision Routing

Building intelligent systems that determine when to accept more action versus being conservative, and when manual review adds value—optimizing the balance between growth and risk management.

Customer & Bet-Quality Intelligence

Creating data products that enable more consistent and economically rational decisions by understanding customer patterns and bet quality signals.

Experimentation & Iteration

Designing feedback loops to quantify trade-offs between margin and volume, risk and growth, enabling continuous strategy refinement backed by data.
